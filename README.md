# 📊 Customer Shopping Behavior & Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze customer shopping behavior, product performance, and marketing effectiveness.

The dashboard helps businesses understand **sales trends, customer segmentation, product insights, and marketing impact** to make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall **sales performance**
* Understand **customer behavior and segmentation**
* Identify **top-performing products**
* Evaluate **marketing strategies (discounts, promotions, subscriptions)**

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset includes:

* Customer details (Age, Gender, Location)
* Product details (Category, Item, Size, Color)
* Transaction data (Purchase Amount, Payment Method)
* Customer behavior (Frequency, Previous Purchases)
* Marketing data (Discount, Promo Code, Subscription)
* Feedback (Review Rating)
* Seasonal data (Season)

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 🏠 Overview

* Total Revenue, Orders, Customers
* Category-wise and Season-wise Sales
* Payment Method Analysis
* Customer Distribution

---

### 👥 Customer Insights

* Customer segmentation (Age, Gender)
* Purchase frequency analysis
* Subscription impact
* Repeat vs New customers

---

### 🛍️ Product Analysis

* Top-performing categories and products
* Top N product analysis with "Others"
* Size and product demand distribution
* Product rating insights

---

### 🎯 Marketing & Operations

* Discount and promo code usage
* Subscription analysis
* Payment behavior
* Marketing impact on revenue

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* Identified **top revenue-generating categories and products**
* Analyzed **customer loyalty and repeat purchase behavior**
* Evaluated **impact of discounts and promotions**
* Compared **subscription vs non-subscription performance**
